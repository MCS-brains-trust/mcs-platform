APES 305 Requirements

The engagement letter must include:

  • Objective and scope of the engagement
  • Responsibilities of the member and client
  • Identification of applicable financial reporting framework
  • Expected form and content of reports
  • Basis of fees and billing arrangements
  • Limitation of liability (if applicable)
